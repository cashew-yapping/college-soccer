Happy New Year everybody!

I’ve decided that, starting this year, I’m posting all of my college soccer yapping in one place. No more Google Docs links! All of my nonsense gets its own little place to live now. This is going to be exactly what I’ve done since 2022, just a bit more organized now.

I’ve got a few posts planned already, including one about the fall transfer portal, so please read that when it comes out because I do have so many things to say. I’m really excited to finally have a space where I can actually make everything feel more put together.

And of course, thank you to everyone for reading my opinions over the last few years and encouraging me to continue doing it. The support really does mean the world to me, and I’m so grateful to have people who actually care about the same random things I do.

Go Cats!
